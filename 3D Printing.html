3D Printing

Nothing much happened this week because we were just printing our chess piece design in 3D. We learn how to configure every feature in Ultimaker Cura. In that session, I learned about profiles, which may be customized to range in quality from lowest quality draft to super fine. The sort of 3D printer that will be utilized may cause this to vary. The goal of that is to speed up printing, but in exchange, the finished product might somewhat deform when printing. In contrast, if the profile setting is set to the greatest level, printing will take longer but the finished product will be extremely fine and detailed.
